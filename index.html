Learn to cycle in an hour

Grasp the basics of cycling in a structured lesson from Coach Edmund,
a professional with over 20 years of experience.

As seen on

everyone can cycle

is the mantra of coach Edmund

With students ranging from age 4 to 65, coach Edmund has been teaching people how to cycle since 2002. It all started when he was trying to teach his daughter to cycle, but his daughter kept falling and getting injured in the process. He then realised he needed to derive a method to teach people to cycle without falling, thus kickstarting his 18 year-long coaching career.
